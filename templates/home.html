About Us

Golden Hearts Family is a value-driven Kenyan based registered CBO . GHF has individuals of vast cultures and backgrounds and embraces inclusion and diversity as this enables us to collaborate and have a variety of thinking to get innovative, efficient and sustainable socio economic-solutions and outcomes out of what we do in alignment with Kenya’s vision 2030 as well as the global Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs). As an organization we aim to: Advocate for mental health awareness in all social sets up and create a platform to speak up (SDG ) Promote access to basic needs for the less fortunate within our communities .
