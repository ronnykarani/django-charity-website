About Us

Golden Hearts Family is a value-driven Kenyan based registered CBO . GHF has individuals of vast cultures and backgrounds and embraces inclusion and diversity as this enables us to collaborate and have a variety of thinking to get innovative, efficient and sustainable socio economic-solutions and outcomes out of what we do in alignment with Kenya’s vision 2030 as well as the global Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs). As an organization we are guided by a value system that consists of: I. Love II. Honesty III. Selflessness IV. Compassion

Mental Health

An outreach program aimed to advocate for mental health awareness within our community through educating people on the importance of psychological, emotional and social well-being. We also aim at ensuring access of professional support for mental health cases within the society.

Mentorship

Guided by the ubuntu philosophy and the African saying “it takes a village to raise a child “ we endeavor to be part of the growth and empowerment of the less fortunate kids through mentorship and promotion of equality by ensuring access to education through the books-drive and the ‘Masomo’ initiatives.

Feeding Program

Through this program we help raise a nutrition healthy community by providing food stuffs for the less privileged and also providing means of achieving a sustainable and a self-sufficient community.
